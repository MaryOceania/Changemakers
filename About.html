Our Story

HackerLab started one weekend when a bunch of friends—mentors, devs, and gamers—were struck by a simple idea: learning should be as addictive as playing. Today, that idea drives a global community where thousands of students rack up XP as they become tech experts.

Inclusion & Diversity

Technology builds the future for everyone, so everyone should help build it. We foster a welcoming space where gender, ethnicity, background, and neurodiversity are not barriers but strengths. Scholarship paths and adaptive challenges ensure no talent is left behind.
